**Chuang Wang** (Student Member, IEEE) received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from Huzhou University, China, in 2023. He has been pursuing a Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science at Jiangnan University since 2023, while also engaging in joint doctoral training at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, from 2025 to 2026. His research interests encompass pattern recognition, neuro-fuzzy systems, intelligent computation, and their practical applications. He has authored or co-authored over 10 academic papers published in leading international journals and conferences, including IEEE Transactions on Fuzzy Systems, IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, Information Fusion, Expert Systems with Applications, International Journal of Intelligent Systems, Crop Protection, EMNLP, BIBM, and the International Conference on Intelligent Computing. He also serves as a reviewer for several international journals and conferences, including AAAI-26, IEEE TIP, IEEE TITS, and IEEE Access, etc.
